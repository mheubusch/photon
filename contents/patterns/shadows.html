Shadows

Usage

When you need to highlight the difference in elevantion between two components you can use shadows.

Shadows can be applied to cards, menus, sidebars and tooltips to name a few.

Style

Based on your needs you can pick three levels of elevation depending on how marked the elevation should be.

Shadow X Y Blur Color Opacity
10 0 1px 4px #0c0c0d 10%
20 0 2px 8px #0c0c0d 10%
30 0 4px 16px #0c0c0d 10%
