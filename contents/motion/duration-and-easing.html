Duration and Easing

A rigidly linear movement looks unnatural to the human eye. Accelerate and decelerate smoothly to help your animation feel more natural.

Animation Curve

In Firefox we use this curve for the majority of animations. Acceleration and deceleration occur asymmetrically to feel natural.

We quickly accelerate at the beginning of the animation and slowly decelerate by the end.

See the curve in action on cubic-bezier.com

Durations

Animations should last from 150ms up to 250ms and have to feel quick and responsive. Shorter animations are also more easily compiled by machines, which helps to achieve this feeling.
